Welcome on my blog

Hi, I'm Zsanna Csengeri. Welcome to my blog, I hope you will enjoy my simple, healthy, and easy recipes. I started cooking when I was 8 years old. The first dish I made, you would never guess, was pancake with jam. The other was a floating island. They were my favorite foods.

Simplicity

I believe that the blessings of simplicity could be true in a lot of different parts in our life. My concept is to make fast, healthy, and colourful food rich in vitamins.

Future

I will provide you from time to time with a piece of useful advise about how you could change your life in a healthy way. Here you are a few first steps. In your cupboard, let's replace the flour with whole meal flour. For cakes you can use almond flour. You can use xylite instead of sugar. Buy fruits rather than chips, chocolate, and biscuits. Change the fizzy drinks for simply water. Ta dah. You are ready to go for a healthier life.
